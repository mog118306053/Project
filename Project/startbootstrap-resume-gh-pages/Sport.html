Rowing

  • I took part in rowing competitions for four years representing my local club Killorglin Rowing Club.
  • I have won 4 All Ireland rowing medals representing both my club and my School.
  • My favourite boat to row in is a Quad. These boats fit 4 rowers and a cox inside them.
  • I enjoy watching rowing competitions on the television such as the Olympics.
  • My favourite rowers are Gary and Paul O'Donovan. They won silver medals representing Ireland at the 2016 Olympics.
