Thailand

  • Thailand is my favourite holiday destination. It has beautiful scenery, tasty food and kind people.
  • I visited Thailand during Christmas 2018/19. I went to both Phuket and the Islands of Phi Phi.
  • Phuket is a beautiful part of Thailand which has a large statue of a Buddha which Thai people worship.
  • Phuket also has beautiful temples such as the Chalong Temple. in which buddhist monks can be found.

Favourite Location in Thailand

  • My favourite location in Thailand is Phi Phi Island.
  • Phi Phi Island is an Island off the coast of Thailand it is a very small island which can only be accessed by boat.
  • There are no roads on Phi Phi Island as the locals use sea taxis and motorbikes to get around. They drive on dirt paths.
